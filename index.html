Current work

Loopzero

An independent research program on early-warning signatures in recursive systems. It pairs a formally specified, Lean-verified obstruction with a matched-false-positive benchmark on public market and recommender data.

The current status is deliberately bounded: the framework is falsifiable and the directional evidence holds, but no detector — the standard methods or my own — has yet reached an accepted operating point under the locked test. So the framework stands; an operational detector is an open problem.

The research program → loopzero.org

The throughline

For over a decade I built immersive and behavior-shaping products — at Meta Reality Labs, and as founder of Pupil before that. The constant was always systems: how they pull behavior in a direction, and what that does at scale. A few years ago I turned the same attention to the inverse question — how recursive systems lose the ability to make progress before they visibly break. That is what Loopzero studies.
